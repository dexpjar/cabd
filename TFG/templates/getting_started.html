Getting Started


G2afe is a suite of bioinformatics tools made by a group with a wide experience into annotations of biological sequences. It intends to be useful for researchers working with whole genomes who are interested in the structural (gene finding) and funcional (protein tagging) annotations of their genomes, also allowing a basic analysis of the results, in particular comparing with other genomes and enrichment of dataset of genes.

The standard workflow that you can run with G2afe, starting for a sequence genome is illustrated in the following picture:



You can discover of the protein-coding genes from your genome and functionally annotate them with Sma3s, which assign gene names, GO terms, and others to every predicted proteins. Finally, you can work with the functional annotation discover interesting features only appearing in your genome.

All G2afe tools are independents and they work as separated modules. You can select a tool, give the required inputs and finally give the results to another different tool, or you can select only one tool and getting the results.

When you select a tool, a form with the required inputs appear, though some of them are optionals. Then, you only have to run the analysis and wait for the results. It is easy to use and the results can make the best of your genome data. So, please, the best way to test is create a new account and proving yourself...
